About McAuley for the 7th

Patrick McAuley is a Republican candidate for U.S. House in Indiana’s 7th District focused on steady, practical leadership. His campaign is centered on safer neighborhoods, attainable housing, and stronger economic opportunity—bringing people together around solutions that deliver results for working families.

Public Safety

Support effective policing while strengthening trust and accountability.

Housing Stability

Expand attainable housing and workforce pathways that help families build long-term security.

Economic Opportunity

Help small businesses grow and make it easier for working families to get ahead.
