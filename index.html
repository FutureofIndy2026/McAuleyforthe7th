Patrick McAuley is a candidate for the U.S. House of Representatives in Indiana’s 7th District.

Since 2015, Patrick has been engaged at every level of government — from grassroots leadership here at home to serving on Capitol Hill as an intern for two U.S. Senators. He has seen firsthand how policy decisions impact real families.

In January 2025, Patrick’s team launched a district-wide survey that generated more than 350 responses from voters across the 7th. The message was clear: families care about affordability, public safety, housing stability, infrastructure, career pathways, and the long-term future of our neighborhoods.

That feedback sparked a year of serious conversations with local and state leaders about the future of this district — conversations rooted in one question: How do we position the 7th District to thrive for the next 30 years?

Over the course of the next few months, Patrick and his campaign team are hosting a series of community town halls. These events will be an opportunity for voters to meet him, his team, and get a first hand introduction to their vision for the 7th District.

We welcome the opportunity to have open dialogue and answer any questions.

This campaign is about focused, determined, hands-on leadership. It’s about building lasting momentum and positioning the 7th District for growth, stability, and long-term success. It’s about leading with energy, accountability, and a clear vision for the future.

The spirit of this district is strong. Its future is worth fighting for.

Patrick and his team are ready to serve.

The time is now.
