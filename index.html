Patrick McAuley is a candidate for the U.S. House of Representatives in Indiana’s 7th District.

Since 2015, Patrick has been inolved in politics in all areas of government ranging from engaging in local grassroots leadership to interning for two U.S. Senators on Capitol Hill.

In January of 2025, his team put out a districe wide survey that recieved over 350 responses from voters on topics ranging from parks and recreation, infrastructure, public safety, housing, career pathways, and the future of families and loved ones in the 7th district.

With this survey, his team immediatley began a year of exploratory meetings with local and state level leaders on running for the 7th District Congressional seat.

Conversations started at the local level by tying in more voices from across the district that led to ideas on how we can progress the district forward for the next 30 years and beyond.

This campaign is about one thing and that's being a focused, determined, hands-on and consistent energetic leader for the 7th District of Indiana.

The spirit of the district is worth fighting for and Patrick and his team are ready to serve.
