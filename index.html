Patrick McAuley is a candidate for the U.S. House of Representatives in Indiana’s 7th District.

Since 2015, Patrick has been engaged at every level of government — from grassroots leadership here at home to serving on Capitol Hill as an intern for two U.S. Senators. He has seen firsthand how policy decisions impact families and their loved ones.

In January 2025, Patrick’s team launched a district-wide survey that generated more than 350 responses from voters across the 7th district. The message was clear: families care about affordability, public safety, housing stability, career pathways, and the long-term future of our communities.

Right now the current leadership in Washington and here in Indiana favors the Republican Party. If we are going to bring change to this district, we need to have the financial resources necessary to provide for infrastructure, public safety, housing, and creating great jobs for the next generation.

Using the relationships he has built over the course of ten years, Patrick and his team will use the current Republican party's platform to bring those resources into the 7th District.

The time is now.
